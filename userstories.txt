CASE STUDY: Pigeon Courier System (PCS)

In USA, there is a Pigeon Courier System which is available in various countries; they collect pet animals, parcels, letter, households, and containers and so on for transporting 
purpose. Their main transport is based on air, water, and road, customer is bill based on weightage and space of occupancy of the goods. The billing system plays a key role; it 
is based on weightage, distance of travel, country and their policies and changes based on country currency. PCS is looking for user-friendly software for their organization;
please guide technical team for design and implementation of software.
